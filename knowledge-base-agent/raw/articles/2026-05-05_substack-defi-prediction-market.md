## 2026: The DeFi shift

**Vấn đề:** Sharp whales kiếm tiền từ long duration markets (6-24 months) nhưng **capital sits idle**.

**Solution:** DeFi composability
- Collateralize token
- Lend out
- Loop around

**"With increasing activity, comes more open interest. With open interest, comes idle positions. With idle positions, comes idle capital."**
