## Pattern Summary

| Session | Typical Top Stories | Typical Đáng Đọc | Tone |
|---|---|---|---|
| Morning | 3-5 (overnight breaking) | 8-10 (balanced) | Fresh, anticipatory |
| Midday | 3-5 (US morning news) | 8-10 (balanced) | Active, developing |
| Wrap | 2-4 (day recap) | 6-8 (lighter) | Reflective, summary |

**Category balance:** Top stories flexible (priority wins), Đáng đọc 50/50 maintained, Narrative always separate.
