# AI, Productivity, and Labor Markets: A Review of the Empirical Evidence

**Source:** International Center for Law & Economics (ICLE)  
**Authors:** Eric Fruits & Kristian Stout  
**Link:** https://laweconcenter.org/resources/ai-productivity-and-labor-markets-a-review-of-the-empirical-evidence/  
**Date:** February 5, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Generative AI đã diffuse nhanh chóng kể từ cuối 2022 — đến cuối 2024, gần **40% U.S. adults** (18-64) báo cáo sử dụng AI tools, nhanh hơn cả PC và internet ở giai đoạn tương đương.

---

## Key Findings

**Micro-level (task/firm):**
- Productivity gains **15-50%+** reductions in task-completion time
- Quality improvements across writing, customer support, software dev, accounting, law, translation
- **"Skill compression"**: Disproportionately large benefits cho less-experienced workers

**Macro-level (2024-2025):**
- **Limited aggregate labor-market disruption** — không có economywide job loss hay wage decline
- Effects concentrated ở **entry-level segments** của highly exposed occupations
- Adjustment through **task reallocation** rather than mass displacement

---

## Macroeconomic Forecasts (divergence due to assumptions)

| Study | Estimate |
|---|---|
| **Acemoglu (2025)** | <0.66% TFP over 10 years (conservative) |
| **Arnon & Smetters (Penn Wharton)** | 10% U.S. GDP affected short-run → 15% over 20 years |
| **Briggs & Kodnani (Goldman)** | +7% global GDP over 10 years (~$7 trillion) |
| **Erdem & Birur** | +$2.48 trillion U.S. GDP by 2030 |

→ Disagreement reflects **assumptions** (task coverage, diffusion speed, organizational redesign) rather than conflicting data.

---

## Productivity J-Curve

Brynjolfsson, Rock & Syverson (2021): GPTs có thể raise long-run productivity even as measured productivity initially stagnates or falls. Early adoption requires investment in organizational redesign, worker training, process change → **gains delayed**.

---

## Competition Policy

- AI lowers minimum viable scale → facilitates startup entry
- Open-source và contracting cung cấp broad access → weakening claims cho mandatory access rules
- Limited support cho regulatory approaches premised on widespread displacement hay durable monopoly
