# When Everyone Has AI and the Company Still Learns Nothing

**Author:** Robert Glaser  
**Link:** https://www.robert-glaser.de/when-everyone-has-ai-and-the-company-still-learns-nothing/

---

## Mô tả

Bài viết phân tích giai đoạn "messy middle" của AI adoption — khi mọi người đều có AI tools nhưng tổ chức vẫn không học được gì.

---

## Vấn đề

- Individual productivity gains ≠ organizational gains
- AI adoption uneven
- The "messy middle": AI use everywhere, uneven, partially hidden

---

## Token-to-Learning vs Token-to-Output

> "What changed because we spent those tokens?"

Better questions: Which loops closed faster? Which decisions improved? Which product ideas killed earlier?

## 3 Capabilities cần thiết

1. **Agent Operations** — control, audit, permissions, runtime visibility
2. **Loop Intelligence** — which loops produce learning
3. **Agent Capabilities** — how useful capabilities flow vào work
