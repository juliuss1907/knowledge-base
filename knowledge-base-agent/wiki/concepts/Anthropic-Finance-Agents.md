# Anthropic Finance Agent Templates

## Mô tả

Anthropic release **10 agent templates** cho financial services — từ research/pitch building đến operations/audit. Đặc biệt: Claude Opus 4.7 đạt **64.37%** trên Vals AI Finance Agent benchmark.

## 10 Templates

### Research & Client Coverage
1. **Pitch builder** — Tạo target lists, comparables, pitchbooks
2. **Meeting preparer** — Chuẩn bị briefs trước calls
3. **Earnings reviewer** — Đọc transcripts, filings, update models
4. **Model builder** — Tạo & maintain financial models
5. **Market researcher** — Track sector, synthesize news

### Finance & Operations
6. **Valuation reviewer** — Check vs comparables & methodology
7. **General ledger reconciler** — Reconcile GL, NAV calculations
8. **Month-end closer** — Close checklist, journal entries
9. **Statement auditor** — Review for audit-readiness
10. **KYC screener** — Assemble entity files, review source documents
