# A New Look at the Economics of AI (MIT Sloan)

**Author:** Daron Acemoglu (2024 Nobel laureate in economic sciences)  
**Link:** https://mitsloan.mit.edu/ideas-made-to-matter/a-new-look-economics-ai  
**Date:** January 21, 2025

---

## Tóm tắt

Acemoglu ước tính tác động của AI đến US economy thấp hơn nhiều so với các forecast khác.

---

## GDP Estimate (10 years)

| Source | Estimate |
|---|---|
| Goldman Sachs | +$7 trillion (7%) |
| McKinsey | $17-25 trillion annually |
| **Acemoglu** | **~1% GDP boost** |

---

## Why Lower Estimate?

- ~20% tasks có thể bị AI exposed
- Nhưng chỉ **~5%** là **profitably performed** (75% còn lại: costs > benefits)
- Productivity gain: **~0.7%** → GDP growth: **~1.1-1.8%**

---

## Caveats

- So far AI chỉ làm "easy-to-learn tasks" (straight line action→outcome, easy to measure)
- "Hard tasks" (như diagnosing persistent cough) → gains limited
- Mismatch: Investment ở large companies, nhưng many AI-compatible tasks ở SMEs
- **Adjustment costs** — organizations need to evolve

→ Only way to boost GDP: increase % tasks impacted OR boost AI capability for new discoveries

---

## Secondary Effects

- **IT & social media:** AI có thể tạo negative welfare (addictive algorithms, security threats)
- **Inequality:** Impact women with low education
- Possible: GDP tăng nhưng overall welfare declines

---

## Reorienting AI

Acemoglu cho rằng AI đang được apply sai:

> "We don't need foundation models that can write Shakespearean sonnets if what we want is reliable information useful for educators, healthcare professionals, electricians, plumbers..."

**Right focus:** Reliable, real-time, context-dependent information cho problem-solving professions.
