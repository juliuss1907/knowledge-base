# AI, Productivity, and Labor Markets: A Review of the Empirical Evidence

**Authors:** Eric Fruits & Kristian Stout (ICLE)  
**Link:** https://laweconcenter.org/resources/ai-productivity-and-labor-markets-a-review-of-the-empirical-evidence/

---

## Mô tả

Review empirical evidence về tác động của Generative AI đến productivity và labor markets. Kết luận: micro-level gains 15-50%+ nhưng macro-level disruption limited.

---

## Key Findings

**Micro-level:**
- Productivity gains **15-50%+** reductions in task-completion time
- Quality improvements across writing, customer support, software dev, accounting, law, translation
- **Skill compression**: Less-experienced workers được lợi disproportionate

**Macro-level (2024-2025):**
- **Limited aggregate labor-market disruption** — không có economywide job loss
- Effects concentrated ở **entry-level segments**
