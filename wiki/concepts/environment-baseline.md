# Environment Baseline

## Definition

Environment Baseline là khái niệm về việc môi trường xung quanh (người, nơi chốn, content) xác định "normal" là gì — set baseline cho mọi thứ khác.

## Key ideas

- **What creates environment:**
  - People you spend time with (family, friends)
  - Places you live, work, spend free time
  - Content you consume (shapes mental environment)
- **People component là quan trọng nhất:** influence từ teachers, parents, friends
- **When everyone around treats mediocrity as normal → it becomes normal**
- **When everyone treats hard work as something to strive for → that becomes normal**
- **Environment compounds:** working for you or against you
