# AI Will Transform the Global Economy (IMF)

**Source:** IMF Blog  
**Link:** https://www.imf.org/en/blogs/articles/2024/01/14/ai-will-transform-the-global-economy-lets-make-sure-it-benefits-humanity

---

## Mô tả

IMF phân tích tác động của AI đến thị trường lao động toàn cầu. Advanced economies ~60% jobs impacted, nhưng cũng có nhiều opportunities hơn.

---

## AI Exposure by Economy

| Economy Type | Jobs Impacted |
|---|---|
| Advanced economies | ~60% |
| Emerging markets | ~40% |
| Low-income countries | ~26% |

---

## Inequality Concerns

AI có thể worsen overall inequality:
- Workers harness AI → productivity & wages tăng
- Workers cannot adapt → falling behind
- Younger workers dễ exploit opportunities hơn older workers
