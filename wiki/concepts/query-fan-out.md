# Query Fan-out

## Definition

Set của concurrent, related queries được AI model generate để request thêm thông tin cho comprehensive response — ví dụ: query về "lawn weeds" → fan-out thành queries về herbicides, natural removal, prevention.

## Key ideas

- **Concurrent related queries**: Model generate nhiều queries liên quan song song
- **Request additional information**: Gather comprehensive data cho complete response
- **Example**: "how to fix a lawn that's full of weeds" → "best herbicides for lawns", "remove weeds without chemicals", "how to prevent weeds in lawn"
- **Used in generative AI search**: Technique của Google AI systems để provide comprehensive answers
- **Improves coverage**: Đảm bảo response cover nhiều angles của topic
