# How to properly manage your multiple interests
## Profound Ideas

**Link gốc:** https://open.substack.com/pub/profoundideas/p/this-is-how-you-can-properly-manage
**Published:** 2026-03-22

## Tóm tắt

Tác giả (22 tuổi, Ireland) chia sẻ framework để integrate nhiều interests mà không feel guilty hay scattered. Problem: productivity advice "pick one thing" bị quadrant biased — chỉ optimize cho 1 quadrant của cuộc sống. Solution: Vision-Anchored Integration — dùng AQAL model + Schema Theory + Spiral Dynamics để biến interests thành system amplify each other.

---

## Vấn đề

- Tác giả từng so sánh mình với productivity gurus → cảm thấy guilty khi đi jiu-jitsu, đọc sách, gặp bạn bè
- Mọi interest đều "stealing time" từ cái khác
- "There is nothing more debilitating than quietly letting curiosity feel like a source of shame."

---

## Tại sao "pick one" cảm giác sai

### Quadrant Bias

Hầu hết productivity advice chỉ optimize cho 1 quadrant:
- Đọc nhiều nhưng socially isolated
- Kiếm nhiều tiền nhưng relationships deteriorate
- Bodybuilder có physique nhưng không hold conversation

**Specialisation có thể làm bạn fragile.**

---

## AQAL Model (Ken Wilber's Integral Theory)

4 perspectives để nhìn cuộc sống:

| Quadrant | Mô tả |
|---|---|
| **Upper-Left (Interior-Subjective)** | Inner world — thoughts, emotions, desires, motivations |
| **Upper-Right (Interior-Objective)** | Biology — brain mechanisms, hormones, physical health |
| **Lower-Left (Exterior-Subjective)** | Culture — shared beliefs, norms, social expectations |
| **Lower-Right (Exterior-Objective)** | Environment — tools, routines, living situation |

Khi "focus on one thing" → chỉ advice từ Lower-Right. Bỏ qua inner drive, cách brain học, cultural pressure.

## 3 Profound Ideas

### 1. Schema Theory — interests nên kết nối

Não lưu trữ information như spider's webs — không phải isolated facts.

- Interests kết nối → strengthen cả network
- Không connect → feel scattered, bị forget

**Cách kết nối:**
| Interest | Kết nối đến |
|---|---|
| Reading | Fuels idea generation, improves thinking, form arguments |
| Writing | Formalized thinking, express ideas |
| Lifting | Cognitive recovery, mental clarity |
| BJJ | Social, creative thinking, discipline |
| Friends | Storytelling → writing, emotional regulation |

### 2. Spiral Dynamics — bạn đang ở đâu

- **Orange** (Achievement): "Pick your most profitable interest" — optimises output, ignores humanity
- **Green** (Exploration): "Just enjoy your interests!" → eventually scattered
- **Yellow** (Integration): "My interests are systematic and interconnected"

Nếu "pick one" cảm giác sai → bạn đã outgrown nó, cần Yellow-level framework.

### 3. Ancient Wisdom Confirmation

- **Plato's tripartite soul**: reason, spirit, appetite — 3 dimensions of single life
- **Aristotle's eudaimonia + arete**: excellence across ALL domains

## 6-Step Framework: Vision-Anchored Integration

### Step 1: Discover your interests
Viết 2 lists:
- Childhood interests (trước khi bị "shoulds")
- Current interests

### Step 2: Map vào 4 Pillars

| Pillar | Ý nghĩa |
|---|---|
| **Learning** | Input — reading, courses, conversations |
| **Creation** | Output — writing, building, making |
| **Movement** | Physical input — training, lifting, martial arts, walking |
| **Value Exchange** | Output to world — teaching, publishing, sharing |

Place 2-4 interests per pillar.

### Step 3: Define vision

> "I am building a life where (outcome) through (pillars)"

Vision như lighthouse — navigate, không exact destination.
