# AI Promises Efficiency, But It's Also Amplifying Labour Inequality

**Source:** The Conversation  
**Link:** https://theconversation.com/ai-promises-efficiency-but-its-also-amplifying-labour-inequality-258772

---

## Mô tả

AI đang reshape work nhưng tác động không đều — amplify existing inequalities. Low-wage workers **14x more likely** bị displaced.

---

## Uneven Impact

**Low-wage/entry-level:**
- **14x more likely** bị displaced so với higher-wage positions
- Women **1.5x more likely** bị pushed into new occupations

**High-wage knowledge workers:**
- **75%** sử dụng AI tools
- **66%** average productivity increase

---

## Complementarity & Education

AI benefit cao nhất khi jobs involve "complementary" tasks — strongly tied to education, highest ở graduate degrees.
