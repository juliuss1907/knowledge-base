# Anthropic ra mắt 10 Finance Agent Templates

**Link:** https://www.anthropic.com/news/finance-agents

---

## Tóm tắt

Anthropic vừa release **10 agent templates** cho financial services, chạy được trên Claude Cowork, Claude Code, và Claude Managed Agents.

---

## 10 Agent Templates

**Research & Client Coverage:**
1. **Pitch builder** — Tạo target lists, chạy comparables, draft pitchbooks
2. **Meeting preparer** — Chuẩn bị briefs trước calls
3. **Earnings reviewer** — Đọc transcripts, filings, update models
4. **Model builder** — Tạo & maintain financial models
5. **Market researcher** — Track sector developments, synthesize news

**Finance & Operations:**
6. **Valuation reviewer** — Check valuations vs comparables & methodology
7. **General ledger reconciler** — Reconcile GL accounts, NAV calculations
8. **Month-end closer** — Chạy close checklist, journal entries
9. **Statement auditor** — Review financial statements cho audit-readiness
10. **KYC screener** — Assemble entity files, review source documents
